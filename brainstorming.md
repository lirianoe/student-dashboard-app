## Favorite Existing Apps - Categorize and Evaluate

### Reminders
   - **Category:** To-Do List
   - **Mobile:** Fully optimized for mobile, allows users to quickly create tasks, set reminders using voice or typing. 
   - **Story:** Allows users create tasks, add tasks details, and schedule task due dates.
   - **Market:** Anyone that would like to keep a digital tracker of their agenda (it being for school, work, or personal use), will find this app handy. 
   - **Habit:** Users can check the app multiple times a day in order to check-mark tasks that are already complete or to log new tasks. 
   - **Scope:** Reminders has come a long way. Many users would prefer using the notes app until it was created, and it has been refining and customizing its user experience with every update. Users can now make categories with icons and colors, add pictures to the task details, connect their calendar, etc. 

### Calendar
   - **Category:** Scheduling / Time Management 
   - **Mobile:** Fully optimized for mobile. The calendar app allows quick event creation, notifications, and now integrates with other apps like email and reminders. 
   - **Story:** Allows users to create and manage events, add event details, and schedule specific times and locations while receiving notifications. 
   - **Market:** Anyone that needs to organize their time (students, professionals, or even families planning daily activities) will find this app useful. 
   - **Habit:** Users can check the app multiple times a day in order to stay updated on upcoming events, deadlines, or appointments. 
   - **Scope:** Calendar has expanded beyond just tracking dates. Users can now share calendars, color-code events, add locations, and connect it with other productivity apps, making it an essential tool for time management. 


### Canvas
   - **Category:** Education
   - **Mobile:** Has a mobile app that allows students to view assignments, grades, and announcements, nonetheless, some features are easier to use on desktop. You can also submit assignments through said app. 
   - **Story:** Allows students and teachers interact through courses by posting assignments, submitting work, grading, and sharing announcements or materials.
   - **Market:** Students and educators in schools, colleges, and universities that need a platform in common for managing coursework will find this app important.
   - **Habit:** Students can check Canvas daily (sometimes multiple times a day) in order to stay updated on assignments, due dates, grades, and announcements.
   - **Scope:** Canvas is a system that continues to grow with features like discussion boards, quizzes, modules, analytics, and integrations with other tools. It has become relevant when accessing resources for academic life for many students.

## New App Ideas - Evaluate and Categorize
1. Student Task Scheduler
   - **Description**: Allows students to create assignment tasks under class categories and stay organized with their coursework. In order to complete tasks, students must submit a file as proof of completion.
   - **Category:** Productivity / Education
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for quickly adding tasks, uploading files, and accessing notes on the go. Students can use their phone camera to upload images or scan documents, and access materials anytime.
   - **Story:** Helps students stay organized by keeping assignments, notes, and resources all in one place. Encourages accountability by requiring proof of completion for tasks.
   - **Market:** Students in high school and college who want a more structured way to manage assignments and class materials would benefit from this app.
   - **Habit:** Students are using the app daily to check assignments, upload completed work, and review notes for their classes.
   - **Scope:** V1 would allow students to create tasks and upload files as proof of completion. V2 would add class categories and note uploads in different formats. V3 would include reminders and deadlines. V4 would add integrations with school platforms and collaboration features.

2. Water Intake Tracker
   - **Description**: Allows users to track their daily water intake and stay consistent with hydration goals. Users can log each drink and receive reminders throughout the day to drink more water.
   - **Category:** Health & Wellness
   - **Mobile:** Mobile is essential for quickly logging water intake throughout the day. Notifications help remind users to drink water, and widgets or quick-add features make tracking fast and easy.
   - **Story:** Encourages users to build healthier habits by staying hydrated. Makes it easy to visualize progress and stay accountable daily.
   - **Market:** Anyone looking to improve their health, including students, athletes, or busy individuals, could benefit from using this app.
   - **Habit:** Users are checking and logging their water intake multiple times a day as they drink water and track their progress toward daily goals.
   - **Scope:** V1 would allow users to log water intake and set daily goals. V2 would add reminders and notifications. V3 would include progress tracking and streaks. V4 would add personalization like custom goals and integrations with health apps.


3. Student Textbook Exchange
   - **Description**: Allows students to buy, sell, and trade textbooks with other students in order to save money on course materials.
   - **Category:** Marketplace / Education
   - **Mobile:** Mobile allows users to quickly list textbooks, take photos of books using the camera, and communicate with other students for buying or selling.
   - **Story:** Connects students together to make textbooks more affordable and accessible. Helps reduce waste by reusing books across semesters.
   - **Market:** College and high school students who want a cheaper alternative to buying new textbooks would find this app useful.
   - **Habit:** Users check the app at the beginning and end of semesters to buy or sell books, and occasionally to browse deals.
   - **Scope:** V1 would allow users to list and browse textbooks. V2 would add messaging between buyers and sellers. V3 would include ratings and reviews for trust. V4 would add payment integration and campus-specific marketplaces.

## Final App Idea Chosen
- Student Task Scheduler is the winner!
